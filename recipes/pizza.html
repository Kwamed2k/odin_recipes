Pizza

The best pizza in town

Pizza is a palatable meal/snack which is basically made from flour dough and consumed by almost people around the world but also serves as a staple food in Europe (precisely Italy).

Originating from the ancient flatbread traditions of the Mediterranean, the modern form of pizza was developed in Naples, Italy, becoming a cheap meal for the working class before gaining royal recognition and international popularity through Italian immigration and global travel.

Bellow is the list of steps to make pizza:

  1. Gather all ingredients. Preheat oven to 450 degrees F (230 degrees C), and lightly grease a pizza pan.
  2. Place warm water in a bowl; add yeast and sugar. Mix and let stand until creamy, about 10 minutes./li>
  3. Add flour, oil, and salt to the yeast mixture; beat until smooth. You can do this by hand or use a stand mixer fitted with a dough hook to make it easier.
  4. Let rest for 5 minutes.
  5. Turn dough out onto a lightly floured surface and pat or roll into a 12-inch circle.
